Getting to Know the Creators:

Akash Jain:
Growing up, Akash was always fascinated by the workings by machines and recreating them. From the child-favorite
TinkerToys to, at a later age, FLL and VEX Robotics, he was in love with the fact that one simple action could
trigger multiple chain reactions simultaneously. Currently, as a freshman in high school, Akash has participated in
various robotics competitions, including the Trinity Fire Fighting Home Robot Competition and the VEX Worlds
Championship from 2015-2016. He also has interests in competitive math and has participated in the AMC 8 and 10
exams, placing in the top 2.5% of the former. Akash is also a proud executive member of his school's student
leadership program and varsity swim team. He is an avid New York Jets fan and likes playing basketball outside
and programming in his free time.

Arnav Nayak:
As a child, Arnav has been very curious and determined. His devotion to technology and logical puzzles
allowed him to develop a love for math, programming and technology. From repairing iPhones to building desktop
computers, his will to discover new learning experiences stop at nothing and for no one. Arnav's love for math
is unparalleled, as shown by his participation on his middle school MathCounts team and the AMC 8 and 10 exam.
He is now a freshman at South Brunswich High School participating in the Robotics Club and the
JSA debate team. He is also an executive member of his school leadership board. In his free time, Arnav likes to
play guitar and program new and unique projects.

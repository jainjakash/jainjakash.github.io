Getting to Know the Creators:

Akash Jain:
Growing up, Akash was always fascinated by the workings by machines and recreating them. From the child-favorite
TinkerToys to, at a later age, FLL and VEX Robotics, he was in love with the fact that one simple action could
trigger multiple chain reactions at the same time. Currently, as a high schooler, Akash has participated in various
robotics competitions. He also has interests in competitive math and is currently preparing for the AMC 10 exam.
Akash is also a proud executive member of his school's student leadership program and varsity swim team. He is an
New York Jets fan and likes playing basketball outside and programming in his free time.
